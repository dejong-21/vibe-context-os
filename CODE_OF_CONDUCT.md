# Code Of Conduct

Be respectful, concrete, and privacy-conscious.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence and clear tradeoffs.
- Protect private sessions, local paths, credentials, screenshots, and logs.
- Keep issues and pull requests actionable.
- Assume contributors may use different AI coding tools and workflows.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Publishing another person's secrets, sessions, local paths, or private artifacts.
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Submitting generated output that hides provenance or bypasses review.
- Encouraging unsafe tool use that exfiltrates local data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy and may block repeat abuse. Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
